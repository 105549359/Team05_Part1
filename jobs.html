Senior Software Engineer

We are seeking a highly skilled and experienced Senior Software Engineer to join our dynamic team. The ideal candidate will be responsible for designing, developing, and implementing complex software solutions.

Salary Overview

Salary Range: $120,000 - $150,000 per year
Reports to: Engineering Manager

Responsibilities

  • Design, develop, and test software applications.
  • Collaborate with cross-functional teams.
  • Mentor junior developers.

Qualifications

Essential
  1. Bachelor's degree in Computer Science or related field.
  2. 5+ years of software development experience.
  3. Proficiency in Java, C++, or Python.
Preferable
  • Experience with Agile methodologies.
  • Cloud computing experience (AWS, Azure, GCP).
  • Master's degree in Computer Science.

DevOps Engineer

We are seeking a detail-oriented and proactive DevOps Engineer to join our team. The successful candidate will help automate and streamline our development and deployment processes.

Salary Overview

Salary Range: $100,000 - $130,000 per year
Reports to: Infrastructure Manager

Responsibilities

  • Manage CI/CD pipelines and automate deployments.
  • Monitor system performance and troubleshoot issues.
  • Ensure infrastructure security and compliance.

Qualifications

Essential
  1. Bachelor's degree in Computer Science, Engineering, or related field.
  2. 3+ years of experience in DevOps or system administration.
  3. Proficiency with tools like Docker, Jenkins, and Kubernetes.
Preferable
  • Experience with cloud platforms (AWS, Azure, GCP).
  • Knowledge of scripting languages (Bash, Python).
  • Certifications such as AWS Certified DevOps Engineer.
